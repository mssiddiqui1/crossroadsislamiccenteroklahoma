About Our Mosque

Established in 2018, Crossroads Islamic Center of Oklahoma is a thriving mosque and a community center frequented by the Muslims residing in the Oklahoma City areas. Our mission is to cater to the needs of the Muslim community in the area by providing a well-maintained facility for prayers and other religious and social community activities.

We work to provide well balanced and authentic information on Islam and Muslims for our community members and for our neighbors of other faiths and affiliations. Non-Muslims are always welcome at our center. You can also arrange a visit by contacting us.

We are a very youth-centric organization and strive to provide many avenues of engagement for our youth such as youth groups, scouting programs, sporting events, and educational and personal development programs.
